Frequently Asked Questions

Creating an event is easy! Simply log in to your account, click on the "Create Event" button in the navigation menu or on the events page. Fill out the event details form with information such as title, description, date, time, location, and any additional details. Once submitted, your event will be reviewed by our administrators and published once approved.

To register for an event, navigate to the event's details page by clicking on the event card from the events list. On the event details page, you'll find a "Join Event" button if registration is still open. Click this button to register. You can view all events you've registered for on your "My Events" page accessible from your profile dropdown.

Yes, you can cancel your registration at any time before the event starts. Go to the event details page or your "My Events" section and click the "Leave Event" button. Your spot will be freed up for others who might want to attend.

If an event is canceled by the organizer or administrator, all registered participants will be automatically notified. The event will be marked as "Canceled" on the platform, and it will appear in your "My Events" section with this status.

You can contact the event organizer through the event details page. Each event has contact information for the organizer, which may include an email address or phone number. Alternatively, you can use our "Contact Us" form to reach out to our support team who can assist in connecting you with the event organizer.

We take data privacy and security very seriously. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We only collect information necessary for the functioning of the platform and never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent. For more details, please refer to our Terms of Service.

Getting Started

1. Creating an Account

To fully utilize our platform, you need to create an account:

  • Click the "Register" button in the navigation bar
  • Fill in your details (name, email, password)
  • Verify your email address
  • Log in using your credentials

2. Exploring Events

You can browse events in several ways:

  • View upcoming events on the homepage
  • Browse all events through the "Events" page
  • Use the search bar to find specific events
  • Check the calendar view for events by date

Creating Events

To create a new event:

  1. Click "Create Event" in the navigation menu or on the events page
  2. Fill out all required information:
    • Event title and description
    • Date and time
    • Location
    • Registration deadline
    • Maximum participants (if applicable)
  3. Submit your event for approval
  4. Wait for admin approval before your event is published
Your event must be approved by an administrator before it appears on the platform.

Managing Event Registration

Joining Events

To register for an event:

  1. Navigate to the event details page
  2. Click the "Join Event" button
  3. Confirm your registration

Leaving Events

If you can no longer attend an event:

  1. Go to the event details page or your "My Events" section
  2. Click the "Leave Event" button
  3. Confirm your decision
You cannot join an event after the registration deadline has passed.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Issue Solution
Cannot log in Check your credentials or use the "Forgot Password" link
Event not showing Your event may still be pending approval
Cannot register for event The event may be full or past the registration deadline
